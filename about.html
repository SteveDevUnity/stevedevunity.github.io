About me

Turning my lifelong love for video games into an actual career wasn’t easy – but it’s been one of the best decisions I’ve ever made. Over the past year, I’ve gone all in on Unity, learning everything from the ground up and finishing my first full game demo, which taught me a ton about both creativity and code.

What excites me most about game development is the journey from an idea to a fully realized experience. It’s a field where art, design, and programming intersect, and I’ve embraced every aspect of the process.

I’ve always been driven by a desire to learn and improve. Whether it’s experimenting with new techniques, exploring cutting-edge tools, or diving into the details of game mechanics, I love the endless opportunities for growth that game development offers.

Outside of work, I enjoy drawing, hiking, and spending time with my family and friends. These hobbies keep me inspired and remind me of the importance of balance and creativity in life.

Game development is more than just a profession for me – it’s the perfect way to combine my skills, interests, and determination. I’m excited to keep pushing boundaries and creating experiences that bring ideas to life.

“The only way to do great work is to love what you do.”
– Steve Jobs

This quote really resonates with me. For me, it’s about finding purpose and drive in what I create – even when the process gets challenging. That mindset has helped me stay motivated throughout my journey into game development.
